Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I trim my trees?

It depends on the type of tree, but generally, once a year is recommended to maintain health and appearance.

What is the best time of year for tree trimming?

Late winter or early spring is usually the best time for most trees, as they are still dormant and can recover quickly.

Can you trim trees in the summer?

Yes, we can trim trees in the summer, but it is essential to consider the specific tree species and its growth cycle.
